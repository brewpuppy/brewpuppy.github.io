Raspberry Pi Purchase

I took the plunge and ordered a Raspberry Pi (Model B) onlong with an accessory kit to get me started. My hope is to use the Raspberry Pi as the controller for a fermentation chamber, and then later as the master to mutliple slave fermentation chambers. But I’m getting ahead of myself; I still have no idea when this will ship. Let the waiting begin!
