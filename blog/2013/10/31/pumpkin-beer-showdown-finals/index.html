Pumpkin Beer Showdown: A Winner Is Chosen

At long last, the time to choose a winner is upon us. I gathered up our favorite beers from the last four rounds and took them to a Halloween party to perform a final tasting experiment: eight people, five beers, one winner.

The finalists were chosen from the top four ranked beers plus one wildcard we wanted to taste again compared to the others. Which pumpkin beer will I be drinking exclusively next Fall? Let’s find out.

The Tasting

Each participant was asked to rate each beer’s Appearance, Smell, and Taste being either a Positive (), Neutral, or Negative () experience. An option to describe the beer in ten words or less was also given. Some people (me!) had difficulty keeping within the ten word limit. After tasting all five beers, the participants were then asked to rank the beers from Best (1) to Worst (5).

Results

Sam Adams – Harvest Pumpkin Ale proved to be the clear winner of the Pumpkin Beer Showdown. Determining the final ranking of the other beers proved to be a bit more difficult. First, for each beer the participants’ rank (1-5) were added together and sorted in descending order to determine the Total Rank.

Total Rank

  1. 19 Sam Adams – Harvest Pumpkin Ale
  2. 22 Blue Moon – Harvest Pumpkin Ale
  3. 24 Schlafly – Pumpkin Ale
  4. 25 O’Fallon – Pumpkin Beer
  5. 30 Buffalo Bill – Pumpkin Ale

Next, for each positive experience a beer would earn 1 points, for each negative experience a beer would earn -1, and for neutral each neutral experience a beer earned 0 points. The points were added together and sorted in ascending order to determine the Overall Experience.

Overall Experience

  1. 9 Sam Adams – Harvest Pumpkin Ale
  2. 8 Schlafly – Pumpkin Ale
  3. 5 O’Fallon – Pumpkin Beer
  4. 4 Blue Moon – Harvest Pumpkin Ale
  5. -2 Buffalo Bill – Pumpkin Ale

Only by averaging the placement of both the Total Rank and the Overall Experience was I able to determine the Final Ranking.

Final Ranking

  1. Sam Adams – Harvest Pumpkin Ale
  2. Schlafly – Pumpkin Ale
  3. Blue Moon – Harvest Pumpkin Ale
  4. O’Fallon – Pumpkin Beer
  5. Buffalo Bill – Pumpkin Ale
