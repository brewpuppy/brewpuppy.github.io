Tasting Notes Hiatus

Right now I am supposed to be finishing the tasting notes of Grainstorm, Boulevard’s latest beer in the Smokestack series. My tasting notes are sitting right next to my keyboard and the text file with my initial notes is still open. This situation has not changed in at least an hour. I didn’t realize how much I was putting this write-up off until I caught myself browsing through pictures of Husky puppies; I just can’t bring myself to finish it.

I enjoy tasting new beers, but for some reason the tasting write-up process has become tedious. While I think my palate is experienced, I believe it lacks the training needed to produce quality tasting notes. For these reasons I have decided to take a break. Unless I have the opportunity to taste a rare beer (e.g. Savor collaboration beers) or gather the needed energy to organize another Showdown, tastings posts will stop. Perhaps when I have passed the Cicerone test I’ll continue regular write-ups.

Going forward I will concentrate on topics more focused on beer-related technology and data. Long story short: Posts will continue but the topics will change, hopefully for the better. Cheers!
